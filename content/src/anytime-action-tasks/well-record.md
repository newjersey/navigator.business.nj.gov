## Record Requirements

- Permit number
- Location of the well
- Local ID (name) of the well
- Diameter of the well
- Depth of the well
- Capacity of the well
- Drilling method
- Grouting method
- Geologic log
- Certification PIN
- Applicable fees paid
- Pumping equipment details, such as license number, if applicable
