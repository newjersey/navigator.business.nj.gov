heading-1: "Qualifying Businesses and Enterprises"
main-text-1: |
  1.  At least 51% owned, operated, and controlled by persons who identify as LGBTQ+ (lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, queer, questioning)
  2.  Independently owned, operated, and controlled business that is generating revenue in New Jersey
  3.  Owner must be competent in the affairs in the business to show operational and managerial control
heading-2: "Registration"
main-text-2: "If you are a LGBTQ+-owned business and seek certification, [register online with the State of New Jersey application](https://www.njportal.com/DOR/SBERegistry)."

main-text-3: "New Jersey State government does not give any priority to M/WBE organizations."
