An FHA or VA Case Number is a unique number assigned to a mortgage loan with the Federal Housing Administration (FHA) or the Veterans Administration (VA). If someone wants to buy a new home you built, you'll need the case number to enroll the home in a warranty plan before the buyer can move in.
