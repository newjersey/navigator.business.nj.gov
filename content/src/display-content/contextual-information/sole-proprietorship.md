Sole Proprietorship is the most basic type of business to establish. You are the only owner of the company and are responsible for its assets and liabilities.

**Advantages**

* Simple and inexpensive to create
* Report profits or losses on your personal tax return

**Disadvantages**

* Personally liable for business debts
* Cannot give/share equity, limiting funding opportunities and potential partners
