A General Partnership is 2 or more people sharing ownership of a single business. The partners manage the business and are responsible for all debts and obligations. The details of this agreement should be written out formally to define the roles of each partner.

**Advantages**

* Simple and inexpensive to create and operate
* Partners report profit or loss on their personal tax returns

**Disadvantages**

* Partners are personally liable for business debts
* Owner is personally liable for partners' or employees' actions
