A [Testing Certification](https://www.nj.gov/dca/codes/forms/pdf_rides/cert_mfg_tstg_tmpl.pdf) confirms that a carnival or amusement ride meets safety standards and is fit for operation. The standards are set by the [American Society for Testing Materials (ASTM)](https://www.astm.org/).
