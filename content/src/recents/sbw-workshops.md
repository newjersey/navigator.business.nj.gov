Get support starting or growing your business during a one-hour virtual workshop, part of National Small Business Week.

During the online workshop, small businesses, startups, and nonprofits can connect with government representatives, small business advocates, and financial lenders and advisors who will offer their expertise on areas of interest that include: financial resources; how to properly register a business in New Jersey; how to certify a business as women-, minority-, or veteran-owned; and ways to access free available technical assistance, federal resources, and mentorship opportunities, among other relevant topics.

Each workshop will cover the same information and will have the same organizations present. Participants can pick 1 of 3 break out rooms to deep dive into. Break out rooms will include the following organizations:

- Small Business Administration, Small Business Development Center, SCORE;
- New Jersey Business Action Center, New Jersey Department of Treasury and Taxation;
- Community Development Financing Institutions.

Local service providers and lender representatives will also attend each workshop to present information about their technical assistance services and resources.
