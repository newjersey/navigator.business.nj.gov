The Garden State C-PACE Program will provide a new form of financing to New Jersey commercial property owners for renewable energy, energy efficiency, water conservation, and certain types of resiliency-related improvements. Learn more at a virtual information session on April 14th, 2023. Feedback on draft C-PACE guidelines will be accepted through May 15th, 2023.
