New Jersey companies with expertise in stormwater management systems can apply for $10 million in grant funding through the Water Infrastructure Investment Plan. Two separate funding programs are available: Technical Assistance and Stormwater Demonstration Projects. Public information sessions on each will be held on August 10th and August 17th, 2022, respectively. The application deadline for Technical Assistance related  grants is August 24th, 2022, and the deadline for Stormwater Demonstration Project grants is September 14th, 2022.
