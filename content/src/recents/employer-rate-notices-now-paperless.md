Employer rate notices are now posted electronically to Employer Access (formerly TWES). The 2024-25 notice is the last that will be mailed to employers.

All employers subject to Unemployment Insurance (UI) are required by law to register on Employer Access to communicate with the Division of UI. In addition to electronic communication and rate notices, Employer Access allows you to see all information specific to your account, such as reports and payments received, any delinquent filings, and outstanding bills.
