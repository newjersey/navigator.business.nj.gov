Businesses and nonprofits can apply for grants ranging from $50,000 to $500,000 to improve food security for residents of the Atlantic City/Ventnor Food Desert Community. Grant recipients will bring nutritious food options to the city as residents await the construction of a full-fledged supermarket. Applications are due by May 15th, 2024.
