Developers and other interested small businesses can learn about the economic outlook for real estate and small businesses in New Jersey by attending a workshop on March 9th, 2023 at 10:00 a.m. at Kean University.

Attendees will learn about current and expected real estate and business trends for 2023, what may affect businesses this year, whether your business may need to pivot, and how to best align business plans for maximum growth and opportunities.

The class is sponsored by the New Jersey Redevelopment Authority (NJRA). Tuition is $75. Use the code NJRATI309 at checkout to receive a 60% discount.
