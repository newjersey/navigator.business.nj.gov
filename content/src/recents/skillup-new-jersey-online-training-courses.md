**Now Open: Available for 180 days after registering**

[**SkillUp New Jersey**](https://www.nj.gov/labor/career-services/education-training/skillup-nj/)\*\* \*\*is a FREE virtual training program to help employees acquire new skills, enhance existing skills, or prepare for certification. Available courses include, but are not limited to: information technology, business analysis, customer service, project management, and digital literacy. Courses vary from basic work readiness skills to tailored training for high-level professionals, with topics such as Microsoft Office, Adobe, Quick Books, Analytical Skills, Data Management/Reporting, Time Management, and Leadership Skills, among many others. In addition, [Metrix Learning](http://nj.metrixlearning.com/landing.cfm) offers training tracks leading to more than 100 industry certifications.

This program is hosted by the New Jersey Department of Labor.
