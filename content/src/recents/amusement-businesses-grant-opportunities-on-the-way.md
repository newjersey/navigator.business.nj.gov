The New Jersey Indoor Amusement Park Grant Program will award one-time grants ranging from $5,000 up to $100,000. Those in [Opportunity Zone](https://nj.gov/governor/njopportunityzones/) eligible census tracts will be eligible for up to $150,000. The funds may only be used for working capital costs.

When the program opens, funds will be disbursed on a first-come, first-served basis. Funds will be available for the application period, which will be at least 30 days, or until funds are exhausted.

This program is currently under development. Updates will be announced in this newsletter as they become available.
