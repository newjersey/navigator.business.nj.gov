Eligible applicants can receive up to $9,375,000 to replace diesel-powered school buses under the U.S. EPA Clean School Bus program.

Private school bus fleets cannot apply directly for funding. However, private fleets that own and operate buses that serve a public school district can enter into a contractual arrangement with an eligible vendor. Eligible vendors will have the capacity to sell zero-emission, CNG or propane-powered school buses (or related charging or fueling infrastructure) to school bus owners and/or arrange financing for such sales. Applications are due by August 19th, 2022.
