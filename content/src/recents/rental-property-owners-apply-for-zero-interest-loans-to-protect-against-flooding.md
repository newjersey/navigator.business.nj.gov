Owners of rental properties with up to seven units requiring rehabilitation or elevation as a result of damages from Hurricane Ida can apply for zero-interest loans from the Small Rental Repair Program. The loans can be used for rehabilitation or mitigation activities such as structural and utility retrofits, grading and slope stabilization, and drainage improvements that are designed to make the rental housing more resilient in future storm events. Assistance may also be provided to make housing accessible for individuals living with disabilities.
