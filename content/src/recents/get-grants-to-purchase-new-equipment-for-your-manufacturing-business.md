Apply for grants ranging from $7,500 to $250,000 through the second phase of the[ New Jersey Manufacturing Voucher Program](https://www.njeda.gov/event/njeda-manufacturing-voucher-program/) (NJMVP). Applications open February 12th, 2024 at 10:00am. Funding can be used to purchase equipment needed to improve operations.
