Teams of entrepreneurs can apply for up to $400,000 in grants, mentorship, and training to jumpstart their startups with the New Jersey Innovation Fellows (NJIF) Program. The NJIF Program supports groundbreaking ideas in life sciences, technology, and other innovation sectors. 

Applications are due by July 5, 2024.
