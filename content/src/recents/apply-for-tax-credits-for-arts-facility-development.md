Small businesses in the arts and cultural arts organizations can apply for tax credits to support facility development and rehabilitation. Projects eligible for the Cultural and Arts Facilities Expansion (CAFE) Program must involve a capital investment of at least $5 million, with opportunities for collaboration and community impact. Tax credit awards can cover up to 100% of eligible project costs. A [mapping tool](https://njeda.maps.arcgis.com/apps/webappviewer/index.html?id=d4e0765e6dee49edabe98357ae6f4407) can assist applicants with identifying equity requirements and other factors that influence application scores. Applications are open through 5:00 p.m. ET on June 6, 2025.
