Property owners, non-profit organizations and other eligible entities interested in supporting the State's clean energy transition with sites, projects, or assets can respond to a Request for Expressions of Interest (RFEI) in the planned **New Jersey Green **NJGF). The NJGF will identify respondents interested in accessing at least $5million in capital to advance clean energy generation and storage, zero-emission transportation, building decarbonization and resiliency, and other clean energy goals.

Responses are due by January 12th, 2024.
