Applications closed October 8th, 2021. 

The New Jersey Small Business Innovation Research (SBIR) and Small Business Technology Transfer Program (STTR) Direct Financial Assistance Program offers grants to support New Jersey companies that are engaged with federal [SBIR/STTR](https://www.sbir.gov/) programs. 

- **Benefits: **Provides $25,000 to $50,000 in fund match to New Jersey-based companies that have received federal SBIR/STTR grants
- **Eligibility: **New Jersey early-stage companies currently engaged with the federal SBIR/STTR programs

[**Learn more about the program.**](https://www.njeda.com/sbir-sttr/)
