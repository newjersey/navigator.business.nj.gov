Female entrepreneurs who are in the early stages of launching companies and seeking funding are encouraged to register for an upcoming Golden Seeds – New Jersey Chapter Office Hourssession. Golden Seeds is a nationwide angel investor network dedicated to investing in women-led startup companies. This event is limited to companies with a female founder or C-level officer. The next session is being held on February 8th, 2023 at 4:00 p.m.

During Office Hours,an experienced Golden Seeds member will lead an overview presentation on the basics of angel funding and the Golden Seeds investment process. This will be followed by 15 minute one-on-one sessions with experienced angel investors who will provide feedback on business ventures and answer questions you might have.
