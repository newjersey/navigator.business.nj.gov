Learn about the range of resources available to small businesses and entrepreneurs in New Jersey at a free webinar during National Small Business Week, April 30th - May 6th, 2023. The Small Business Services Team at the New Jersey Economic Development Agency (NJEDA) will host three separate sessions for [**North Jersey**](https://www.njeda.com/event/njeda-small-business-week-show-me-the-resources-webinar-for-northern-new-jersey/?utm_campaign=20230420digest&utm_medium=email&utm_source=govdelivery)**, **[**Central and South Jersey**](https://www.njeda.com/event/njeda-small-business-week-show-me-the-resources-webinar-for-central-south-new-jersey/?utm_campaign=20230420digest&utm_medium=email&utm_source=govdelivery)**,** and for the [**Spanish-speaking business community**](https://www.njeda.com/event/semana-nacional-de-la-pequena-empresa-de-la-njeda-muestrame-los-recursos/?utm_campaign=20230420digest&utm_medium=email&utm_source=govdelivery)**.**
