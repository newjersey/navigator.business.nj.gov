New Jersey businesses that file Corporation Business Tax or Insurance Premiums Tax can bid for tax credits at a discount of up to 15 percent at a tax credit auction now underway. The Food Desert Relief Tax Credit Auction will fund programs aimed to improve and increase access to healthy and affordable food throughout New Jersey's 50 [**Food Desert Communities**](https://www.njeda.gov/njeda-board-approves-nj-food-desert-communities-designations-list/?utm_campaign=20230817digest&utm_medium=email&utm_source=govdelivery) (FDCs).
