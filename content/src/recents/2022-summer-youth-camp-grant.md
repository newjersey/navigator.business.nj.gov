The [2022 Summer Youth Camp Grant](https://www.childcarenj.gov/ChildCareNJ/media/media_library/FINAL_2022_Summer_Youth_Camp_Grants.pdf) application is now available, with grants amounts ranging from $7,500 - $25,000. **Applications will be accepted through August 5th, 2022.**

### Eligibility:

The camp program must have an active 2022 Summer Youth Camp Certificate of Approval, comply with state and local health and safety requirements, the Child Care Development Block Grant (CCDBG) health and safety requirements

Priority will be given to facilities and programs that have not received any American Rescue Plan (ARP) Stabilization Grant funding.

### Benefits:

Grant amounts range from $7,500 to $25,000.
