Apprenticeships can help identify new talent, particularly in tight labor markets and during periods of low unemployment. New Jersey businesses can train new workers with support from the following programs:

- Successful [Growing Apprenticeship In Nontraditional Sectors (GAINS)](https://www.nj.gov/labor/assets/PDFs/Grants/FY24/FY2024_001_GAINS_NGO_FINAL.pdf) applicants can receive up to $12,000 in wage reimbursement funds per individual apprentice.
- Competitive proposals for [Pre-Apprenticeship in Career Education (PACE)](https://www.nj.gov/labor/assets/PDFs/Grants/FY24/FY2024_001_%20PACE_NGO_Final.pdf) grants can receive as much as $12,000 per trainee.

Prospective GAINS applicants are strongly encouraged to register for a technical assistance workshop that will be held online on November 20th, 2023.
