501c non-profit organizations can apply for matching grants worth up to $750,000 to plan and implement the management and preservation of historic sites through the Preserve New Jersey Historic Preservation Fund. Prospective applicants are strongly encouraged to read the[ grant guidelines](https://www.nj.gov/dca/njht/documents/programs/preservenj/2023%20Grant%20Guidelines%20FINAL.pdf) to determine if their proposal is eligible for funding. Applications are due April 19th, 2023.
