[Premier Business Services](https://www16.state.nj.us/NJ_PREMIER_EBIZ/jsp/home.jsp) (PBS) is a secure system to allow business owners or their authorized representative to access information and services important to their business.
The system shares a single login with the [MyNewJersey Portal](https://my.state.nj.us/openam/UI/Login).

In order to sign up for PBS, you must:
• Setup a MyNewJersey user name and password
• Add a PBS account to your MyNewJersey account
• Add your business to your PBS account
