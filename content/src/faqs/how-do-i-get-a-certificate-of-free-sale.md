Certificates of Free Sale assist those who sell domestically produced food, drug, cosmetic, and medical device products internationally.

The certificates are issued through the New Jersey Department of Health: [New Jersey Certificate of Free Sale Project and Imports/Exports](https://www.state.nj.us/health/ceohs/food-drug-safety/cert-free-sale/).
