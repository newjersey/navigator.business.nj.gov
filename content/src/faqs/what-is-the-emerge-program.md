<a>**Emerge**</a>

The Emerge Program provides per-job tax credits to projects that invest private capital into the state and create new good-paying jobs (or retain a large number of good-paying jobs) in Governor Murphy’s priority sectors and in targeted communities across the New Jersey.

- **Benefits: **Annual per-job tax credits up to $8,000 per job per year. Jobs that are covered by a labor harmony agreement are eligible for an additional $1,000 bonus over the capped amounts.
- **Eligibility: **Companies that meet the program minimums for new job creation and/or retained jobs, are located in an [eligible incentive location](https://urldefense.com/v3/__https://njeda.maps.arcgis.com/apps/webappviewer/index.html?id=8fed69ed4a664ec6b76f2a6ab633444c__;!!J30X0ZrnC1oQtbA!anFOJ_a201N-IS8DcK_JMEMEwDnLd-ZFAlH8FcXYN1KKyogjJYl79pVZSl6vwXZXqZetcTA$), and can demonstrate the award of tax credits is a “material factor” in their decision to locate in New Jersey versus an out of state alternative location.
- **Learn More: **[https://www.njeda.com/emerge/](https://urldefense.com/v3/__https://www.njeda.com/emerge/__;!!J30X0ZrnC1oQtbA!anFOJ_a201N-IS8DcK_JMEMEwDnLd-ZFAlH8FcXYN1KKyogjJYl79pVZSl6vwXZX40R_q4Y$).
