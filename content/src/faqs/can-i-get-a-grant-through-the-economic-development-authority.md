[**Small Business Lease Assistance Program**](https://njeda.com/leaseassistance/)**:** Reimbursement of a percentage of annual lease payments to for profit businesses and non-profit organizations in eligible areas that plan to lease between 500 and 5,000 square feet of new or additional market-rate, first-floor office, industrial or retail space for a minimum 5-year term.

- Eligible cities: AC, Bridgeton, Camden, Jersey City, Millville, Mt. Holly, Passaic, Paterson, Phillipsburg, Plainfield, Salem, Trenton, Vineland

[**NJ Ignite**](https://njeda.com/njignite/)**:** Supports early stage technology and life science companies in New Jersey by providing rent support grants that will enable these companies to operate from a collaborative workspace. In addition, this program provides collaborative workspaces with a new tenant attraction tool.
