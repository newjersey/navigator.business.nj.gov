Depending on how your business is organized, forms can be used to amend your charter documents.

Forms to amend certificates of incorporation be found at the [New Jersey Department of Treasury - Amend Certificates of Incorporation](https://www.state.nj.us/treasury/revenue/amendcerts.shtml).

Contact the [New Jersey Business Action Center](http://business.nj.gov/plan/business-resources/business-action-center) for more information about moving or expanding your business.
