There are 566 municipalities in New Jersey. Some municipalities employ economic development professionals and administer business programs and incentives in addition to those offered on the state level. For a listing of available municipal (and 21 county) websites, visit [www.nj.gov/nj/gov/county/.](http://www.nj.gov/nj/gov/county/)
