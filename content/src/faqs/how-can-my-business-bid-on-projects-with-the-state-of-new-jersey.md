You must be a registered Vendor with [NJSTART](https://www.njstart.gov/bso/) if you want to be an Economic Development Authority vendor or bid on projects for EDA.

To learn more about bidding opportunities with EDA, visit [this link](https://njeda.com/bidding/).

To learn more about NJSTART, Contact Nichole Eckart at (609)-858-6927.  

For inquiries related specifically to real estate bidding, email [qared@njeda.com](mailto:qared@njeda.com).
