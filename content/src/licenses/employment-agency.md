Your employment agency license and agent license expires every year.

### Renewal Requirements
- `Surety bond|surety-bond` number and premium paid receipt 
- Current license number
- `Registered agent|registered-agent` number
- Criminal history of the applicant, company officers, directors, or principals, if applicable
- Applicable fees paid

:::callout{ showHeader="true" headerText="" showIcon="false" calloutType="conditional" }
- A renewed employment agency license and agent license are valid for one year. It will be sent to your mailing address on file.
