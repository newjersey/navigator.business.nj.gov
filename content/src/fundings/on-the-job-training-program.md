## Eligibility

* Employers must be registered in New Jersey
* Employers must train workers at their job site, using workplace tools or equipment
* New hires must be unemployed New Jersey residents or residents facing job loss
* Businesses should offer full-time, long-term jobs that pay above the State minimum wage

:::largeCallout{ showHeader="true" headerText="Benefits" calloutType="conditional" amountIconText="" filingTypeIconText="" frequencyIconText="" phoneIconText="" emailIconText="" }

Businesses can get back 50% of the wages for training new workers, up to $10,000 or 26 weeks for each employee. Employers may also get no-cost, six-month, $25,000 federal bonds for hiring public assistance recipients.

:::
