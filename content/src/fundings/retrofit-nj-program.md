## Eligibility

* Building owners or tenants can apply. The building must already exist and be used as a commercial, industrial, or institutional building
* Tenants must have owner approval and a valid lease for one year after the end of the construction project
* If applying to retrofit an institutional building, it must serve a non-profit or public purpose, like a library, hospital, or school
* Must be in good standing with the New Jersey Department of Labor and Workforce Development (NJDLWD) and NJ Department of Environmental Protection (NJDEP)
* Must provide a current tax clearance certificate

:::largeCallout{ showHeader="true" headerText="Benefits" calloutType="conditional" amountIconText="" filingTypeIconText="" frequencyIconText="" phoneIconText="" emailIconText="" }

The minimum grant award size is $2,500,000. For projects with Thermal Energy Networks (TENs), the maximum grant award size is $12,500,000 per project/Employer Identification Number (EIN). For all other projects, the maximum grant award size is $10,000,000 per project/EIN.
