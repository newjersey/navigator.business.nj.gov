section-1: "‍**‍**The NJ Small and Micro Business Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) Access Program is a public-private partnership that helps businesses and non-profits protect the health and safety of their employees and customers.​ Through the program, all New Jersey-based businesses and non-profits are eligible for 10% discounts on PPE purchases made through approved online retailers, including Boxed, Staples, and Office Depot. [Learn more and purchase discounted PPE](https://covid19.nj.gov/ppeaccess).\n\nBusinesses can also utilize a free online PPE planning tool to estimate how much PPE they will need in the coming months. The planning tool is available at\_[https://covid19.nj.gov/ppeaccess](https://covid19.nj.gov/ppeaccess).\n\nIf you are interested in becoming an approved online retailer, or are a NJ-based PPE manufacturer or wholesaler, see\_[https://covid19.nj.gov/ppeaccess\_](https://covid19.nj.gov/ppeaccess)for more information on how you can participate."
