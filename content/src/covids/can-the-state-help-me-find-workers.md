section-1: |-
  Many businesses across New Jersey have urgent hiring needs during the COVID-19 emergency and are looking to hire thousands of workers, including those who lost their jobs or have had hours reduced as a result of COVID-19. The State has created a COVID-19 Jobs and Hiring Portal to connect talent with opportunities in their communities at [jobs.covid19.nj.gov.](http://jobs.covid19.nj.gov/)

  If your business is facing urgent staffing shortages due to COVID-19, you can connect with these workers seeking New Jersey job opportunities. If your business is currently and urgently in search of talent, [submit your business for inclusion via our intake tool for businesses that are hiring](http://jobs.covid19.nj.gov/intake).

  Note: If your business is an employer hiring for more than 5 positions, or hiring for a specific position at more than 5 locations, we recommend you complete the intake process via an alternative Excel form. An Excel spreadsheet that can be downloaded at [jobs.covid19.nj.gov/intakeexcel](https://jobs.covid19.nj.gov/intakeexcel) and then emailed to [covid19jobs@njeda.com](mailto:covid19jobs@njeda.com)
