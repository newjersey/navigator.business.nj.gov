Cannabis licenses are reviewed on an ongoing basis and you can apply at any time.
- - -

### Application Needs

- []{business-plan} Business Plan
- []{business-name} Business Name
- []{business-reg-cert} A `Business Registration Certificate|business-reg-certificate` 
- []{ein} `EIN|ein` and `NJ Tax ID|tax-id` or SS# (if Sole Proprietorship or General Partnership)
- []{poi} Information on all `Persons of Interest|poi-cannabis` 
- []{eoi} Information on all `Entities of Interest|eoi-cannabis` 
- []{business-local} The proposed location of the cannabis business
- []{landord-contact} Name and contact information for the proposed landlord, if applicable
- []{entity-disclosure-forms-cannabis} [Entity Disclosure Forms](https://www.nj.gov/cannabis/documents/businesses/personal-use/CRC%20Entity%20Disclosure%20Form%20Fillable.pdf) for the license applicant and all Entities of Interest
- []{personal-disclosure-forms-cannabis} [Personal History Disclosure Forms](https://www.nj.gov/cannabis/documents/businesses/personal-use/Personal%20History%20Disclosure%20Form.pdf) for all Persons of Interest
- []{cert-formation} A `Certificate of Formation|certificate-formation` for the license applicant and all Entities of Interest
- []{service-contracts-cannabis} All management services contracts and financial source agreements, whether proposed or executed
- []{vendor-contracts-cannabis} Any Vendor-Contractor agreements if the Vendor-Contractor qualifies as an Entity of Interest
- []{affidavit-cannabis} [Completed affidavit](https://www.nj.gov/cannabis/documents/businesses/personal-use/Cannabis%20Business%20Applicant%20Affidavit%20Waiver%20Release.pdf)
- []{authorized-release-cannabis} An authorization to release all information pertaining to the license holder
- []{liablity-waiver-cannabis} A waiver of liability for any damages to the Person of Interest that may result from any disclosure or publication of any information acquired during the licensing process
- []{site-plan-cannabis} A site plan of the proposed location including a floor plan
- []{location-description-cannabis}A description of the suitability of the location, which may include renderings, architectural plans, or engineering plans
- []{copy-lease}Copy of the lease agreement or letter of intent demonstrating that the applicant will have final site control of the premises upon application approval
- []{local-codes-compliance-cannabis} Evidence of location compliance with local codes and ordinances
- []{zoning-compliance-cannabis} Zoning approval in the form of a letter or affidavit from appropriate municipal officials stating that the location will conform to local zoning requirements allows for activities related to the operations of the proposed cannabis business and lists any variances granted for the business
- []{local-gov-resolution-cannabis} A resolution adopted by the municipalityâ€™s governing body, or where the municipality has no governing body, a written letter of support from the municipalityâ€™s executive stating that the applicant is compliant with [Section VIII of Notice of Application Acceptance](https://www.nj.gov/cannabis/documents/businesses/personal-use/Final%20Notice%20of%20Application%20Acceptance.pdf)
- []{labor peace-agreement-cannabis} Your application can gain additional priority by providing an attestation from a labor organization indicating that you two have entered a labor peace agreement (this is not required and no micro businesses do not gain additional priority for this)
- []{application-fees-cannabis} Application fees
- []{dob} **`Diversely-Owned Businesses|diversely-owned-business-cannabis` Only:** Copy of Minority or Women-Owned Business Enterprise Certification or Disabled Owned Veteran Certification with the NJ Department of Treasury
-[]{impact-zone} **Impact Zone Businesses Only:** Proof that the business meets the `requirements of an Impact Zone Business|impact-zone-business-cannabis` 
- []{social-equity-biz}**Social Equity Businesses Only:**
  A [notarized statement](https://www.nj.gov/cannabis/documents/businesses/personal-use/Certification%20of%20SEB%20FINAL%2012.13.21.pdf) by the owner(s) that they meet the criteria for a `Social Equity Business|social-equity-business-cannabis` and documentation (such as a tax return) to prove residency
- []{microbiz-cannabis} **Microbusiness Only:** A completed attestation (provided in your online application) that the business will comply with the `requirements for a microbusinesses|microbusiness-cannabis`
